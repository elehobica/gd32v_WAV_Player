Manual

* Button Control
 Please connect android headphone with 3 buttons

1. Power On/Off
 Long push Center button

2. FileView Mode
 Up/Down button to go up/down
 1 click to get into the folder
 1 click to play the wav file (go to Play Mode)
 2 click to go parent folder
 3 click to random album play (go to Play Mode)

3. Play Mode
 1 click to pause/play
 2 click to stop (go back to FileView Mode)

* Auto Power Off condition
 3min idle in FileView mode (except for Auto Rndom Play condition)
 3min pause in Play mode

* Auto Random Play condition
 1min after whole files play finished
